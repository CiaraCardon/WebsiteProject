How to Make Great Cookies

  1. Ingredients
    • 2 cups of flour
    • 1/2 t. Baking Soda
    • 1/2 t. salt
    • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup white sugar
    • 1 tbsp. Vanilla extract
    • 1 egg
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  2. Instructions
    • Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
    • Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside
    • In a medium bowl, cream together the melted butter, brown sugar and white sugar. Beat in vanilla, egg, egg yoke until light and creamy.
    • Mix in the sifted ingredients. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon
    • Roll in a scant half-cup of dough into a ball. Placed formed dough onto cookie Sheet. Bake, reversing poistion of cookie sheets halfway through baking, until cookies are light golden brown (approximately 11-14 minutes)
